Frequently Asked Questions

What if my client replies to the email?
All emails have your email address as the reply-to, so any responses go directly to you. You handle the conversation naturally, and you can mark the invoice as paid or stop chasing anytime.
Will this damage my client relationships?
Our emails are carefully written to be polite and professional. They sound like you wrote them personally. The tone is friendly but firm, never aggressive. Many freelancers find clients appreciate the gentle reminders.
How do I stop chasing if I get paid?
Simply click "Got Paid" on your dashboard and we'll immediately stop all follow-ups for that invoice. You can also click "Stop" if you want to stop chasing for any other reason.
Can I customize the message tone?
Currently, we use a proven formula that escalates from friendly to firm over multiple follow-ups. Custom tones are on our roadmap! For now, the emails are designed to work for most professional relationships.
Is it really free forever?
Yes! We're in early access and it's completely free while we build and improve the product. We may introduce paid features later, but the core invoice chasing will remain free.
